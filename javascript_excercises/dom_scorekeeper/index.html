DOM Score Keeper

Have 2 players. Keeps track of player 1 score and player 2 score.
Load up the page and it tells us we are playing to 5 points.
Click on Player 1 button and it adds a point to player1 score.
Click on Player 2 button and it adds point to player2 score.
Whoever gets to 5 the score turns green and you can't add to the score anymore.
Hit the reset button, it removes the score and starts fresh.
Another feature is we can specify the max playing points
